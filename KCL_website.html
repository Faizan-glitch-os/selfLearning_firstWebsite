Kanjar Cricket League was founded by Faizan Ahmad and Farooq Hameed in the year 2023. This was happened due to
complications with the Rahman Brothers namely Ibrahim Rahman and Ali Shayan, leading to the departure from
Pecharh Cricket League.Farooq Hameed suggested the name Kanjar Cricket League as He stated that we are all
Kanjars playing in it. This name was warm heartedly accepted by every member. Aizaz Ahmad gently sponsored
the league and gave them personal space so they can peacefully conduct their league. Their younger brothers
Zalan Ahmad, Shehbaz Ahmad and Anas Hameed also played a great role in conducting every event. UbaidUllah
cousin to every member of Kanjar Cricket League also plays vital role in conducting the event. Other people who
helps sometimes are Ibrahim, Mustafa, Ibrar Ahmad and Bilal Ahmad.
